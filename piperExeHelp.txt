  --noise-scale, --noise_scale NOISE_SCALE
                        Generator noisec ontrols the pitch variation and vocal roughness.
		    Higher values increase the amount of random noise added to the voice,
			which can make it sound more expressive or, if too high, more degraded/crackly.
		    Lower values make the voice sound more clear and uniform (sometimes robotic).
			Default is often 0.667.
  --noise-w-scale, --noise_w_scale, --noise-w, --noise_w NOISE_W_SCALE
                        Phoneme width noise controls the variability and "jitter" in the rhythm
		    and pauses between words/phonemes.		>1.0 (Higher): Introduces more extreme pauses and stutters, making the speech sound very erratic or expressive.
		    Default is 0.333 (or similar)				<1.0 (Lower): Makes the speech cadence more uniform and machine-like.
