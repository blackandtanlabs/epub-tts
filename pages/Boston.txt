Car: Cah
Park: Pahk
Idea: Idear
Water: Watah
Coffee: Caw-fee (sometimes "Cawfee")
Remote (Clicker): Clicka
Drinking Fountain (Bubbler): Bubblah
Rubber Band (Elastic): Elastics (with an 'a' sound at the end)
Mother (Mum): Mum (or "Mawm")
Living Room (Parlor): Pah-luh
Jeans (Dungarees): Dungarees (with a hard 'g')
Milkshake (Frappe): Frah-ppeh 

Key Features of the Accent:

    Non-rhoticity: Dropping 'r' sounds at the end of words, but adding them to
    words that start with a vowel sound (like 'idea').
